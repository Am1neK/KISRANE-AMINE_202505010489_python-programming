1.1 Define the problem statment 
Create a Python program to automatically calculate a custumor cafe bill based on the quantity of Coffee, tea and sandwich ordered and display the receipt 

1.2 What are the Input ?
Customer name
Coffee quantity
Tea quantity
Sandwich quantity

1.3 What are the Outputs?
Customer receipt 
Total price RM

1.4 What would be the typical process flow ? 
Enter the customer name 
Enter the coffee quantity 
Enter the tea quantity
Enter the sandwich quantity 
Calculate total bill
Display receipt

1.5 What are the constraints?
quantity cannot be negative 
Input must be numbers for quantities
fixed prices :
coffee = RM8.5
tea = RM6.00
sandwich = RM12.00

2 Decompose the problem into smaller tasks 
Get customer information 
Get quantities of ordered items
Calculate total amount 
Generate and print receipt 
Display final output 

3 Pseudocode
START

INPUT customer_name
INPUT coffee_quantity
INPUT tea_quantity
INPUT sandwich_quantity

SET total =
(coffee_quantity × 8.50)
+
(tea_quantity × 6.00)
+
(sandwich_quantity × 12.00)

DISPLAY receipt
DISPLAY customer name
DISPLAY quantities
DISPLAY total

END
